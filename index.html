Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite Velion to my server?

+

To invite Velion to your server, simply click on the "Invite Velion" button at the top of this page and follow the Discord authorization process.

Is Velion compatible with other bots?

+

Yes, Velion is designed to work alongside other Discord bots without conflicts. However, we recommend checking for command overlaps to ensure smooth operation.

Can I customize Velion's prefix?

+

You can easily change Velion's command prefix to suit your server's needs using the !setprefix command.

How often is Velion updated?

+

We release updates for Velion on a bi-weekly basis, including new features, improvements, and bug fixes. Major updates are announced on our Discord server and website.
